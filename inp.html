About Eduquity

Eduquity is a leading and pioneer assessment organization. The concept of online examinations and assessments was introduced in India by Eduquity. We were the first organisation to conduct large-scale examinations in the country.

Our Success Mantra

Assessment is at the core of Eduquity, be it for any evaluation system - academic, corporate, or government. With over 25 years of experience in delivering online assessments internationally, Eduquity has become the quintessential expert at conducting large scale, high-stakes online examinations across centres in India and abroad.
We have a comprehensive and unparalleled understanding of region-wise differences in infrastructure and internet connectivity issues. We have also successfully demonstrated strict adherence to security standards desirable for such high-stakes examinations.We are the only assessment company in India to have zero failure rate till date.
