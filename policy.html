Cancellation & Refund Policy

At Riddhi Stack IT Solutions, we aim to ensure complete satisfaction with our services. However, if you wish to cancel a service or request a refund, the following terms apply:

Cancellation Terms

  • Cancellations must be requested within 7 days of service initiation.
  • After 7 days, cancellations may not be eligible for a refund.
  • For subscription-based services, cancellation requests must be made before the renewal date to avoid future charges.

Refund Terms

  • Refunds will be processed within 10-15 business days after approval.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used during the transaction.
  • No refunds will be provided for partially used services.

Exceptions

In cases where a service is delayed due to unforeseen circumstances or technical issues, refunds may be considered at the sole discretion of Riddhi Stack IT Solutions.
